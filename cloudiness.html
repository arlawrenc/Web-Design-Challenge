Comparison: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloudiness

Warm, moist air converges at lower altitudes near the equator, rising and cooling and, therefore holding less moisture.

These changes cause water vapor to condense into cloud particles and produces a dependable band of thunderstorms in an area known as the Inter Tropical Convergence Zone (ITCZ).
